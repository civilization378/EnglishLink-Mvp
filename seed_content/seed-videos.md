## 1. Ordering Coffee

场景：daily  
难度：A1  
视频地址：暂用测试视频  

英文字幕：
I'd like a small coffee, please.

词汇释义：
- small: not large
- coffee: a hot drink

理解题：
What does the speaker want?

选项：
A. Tea
B. Coffee
C. Water

正确答案：
B

解释：
The speaker says "I'd like a small coffee", so the answer is Coffee.

## 3. Meeting a Friend

场景：social  
难度：A1  
视频地址：暂用测试视频  

英文字幕：
Long time no see!

词汇释义：
- long time: a long period
- see: meet or look at someone

理解题：
What does the speaker mean?

选项：
A. They meet often
B. They haven't met for a long time
C. They are saying goodbye

正确答案：
B

解释：
"Long time no see" means they haven't met for a long time.
